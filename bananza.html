About Us!

BANANZA's goal is to educate kids on proper hygiene practices that can help shape them. These basic yet extremely important skills will aid these children in choosing safe life practices as they grow up. We aim to do this by providing children with enjoyaable games that are additionally educational. The Monkey Business team hopes to create an influential experience on these impressionable children and leave positive long-effects on their lives.
