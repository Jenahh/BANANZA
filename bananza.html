About Us!

BANANZA's goal is to educate kids on basic yet important nutritional and hygienic practices. We aim to do this by providing children with enjoyable games that are informative. The Monkey Business team hopes to create an influential experience on these impressionable children and leave positive long-term effects on their lives.

Learn more about our games below!

Kindergarten

Mask Up

Protect yourself in a large crowd by putting masks on the people. Move quickly or they'll disappear!

Grade 1

Snake (Monkey Edition)

Eat your bananas in order to obtain your healty dose of potassium! Use the arrow keys to move. Go out the box and you lose!

Grade 2

Matching Munchies

How well do you know your nutritional groups? Match the category with the corresponding food/drink.

Grade 3

Bye Bye Stinky in the Sinky!

Simulate brushing your teeth, washing your hands, and drying them to practice having a clean routine!

Grade 4

Big Kid Biology

Grab a friend and duke it out! Who knows the most about their human body? Don't forget this!

Grade 5

Immune Defense

Simulate your immune system with a tower-defense game fending off against nasty bacteria!
